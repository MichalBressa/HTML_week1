Berserk

Well manga is a kind of book, isn't it? Anyway, Berserk is the dark fantasy story of the badass Guts. All he knows since birth is sword and blood. He goes through countless battles against humans or demons surviving because of his sword-wielding skills and boiling rage. Definitely worth checking out for story and characters.
